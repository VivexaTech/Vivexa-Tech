Making Professional Web Development Accessible to Everyone

Vivexa Tech was founded with one clear goal: to make professional, high-quality websites affordable for new businesses.

We noticed that many startups in Gurugram and beyond had to choose between cheap, ineffective templates and overly expensive custom designs. Our mission is to provide the perfect middle ground: custom-built websites that look amazing, perform great, and are offered at a price that startups can actually afford. We are here to be your partners in growth.
