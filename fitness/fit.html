FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is Synergy Fitness Hub open 24/7?
Yes, our gym offers 24/7 access to all members, so you can work out whenever it best suits your schedule.
Do you offer personal training?
Absolutely! We have a team of certified personal trainers who can create customized workout plans and provide one-on-one guidance to help you reach your specific fitness goals.
What kind of classes do you offer?
We offer a wide variety of group fitness classes including Yoga, Pilates, Zumba, HIIT, Spinning, and more. Check our schedule for the latest class offerings!

Yes, we offer a free 1-day pass so you can experience our facilities and classes firsthand before committing to a membership. Fill out the form in our CTA section to claim yours!

Your safety is our priority. We have 24/7 surveillance, emergency call buttons, and regular cleaning protocols in place. Our equipment is regularly maintained.
