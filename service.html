Services to Get Your Business Online

We offer everything a new business needs for a professional and effective online presence, all at a budget-friendly price.

Affordable Website Design

We build professional, modern websites that make a great first impression. Our focus is on creating a clean, fast, and reliable online home for your business that you can be proud of, tailored to your specific needs and budget.

  • Professional Multi-Page Sites: Get a complete website with all essential pages like Home, About, Services, and Contact.
  • Easy Content Management: We can build your site on user-friendly platforms like WordPress, allowing you to update content yourself.
  • Essential Feature Integration: We'll add key features like contact forms, Google Maps, and social media links.

User-Friendly & Mobile-First Design

A great website must be easy to use. We design with your customer in mind, ensuring the layout is intuitive and looks perfect on any device, from desktops to smartphones. This creates a positive experience that keeps visitors engaged.

  • Clean & Modern Layouts: We design visually appealing websites that are easy for your customers to navigate.
  • Intuitive Navigation: We structure your site logically so visitors can find what they're looking for without frustration.
  • Looks Great on All Devices: Your website will be fully responsive, providing a seamless experience on phones, tablets, and computers.

Simple E-commerce Setup

Ready to sell your products online? We make it easy. Our Premium Plan includes setting up a simple, secure, and easy-to-manage online store, so you can start generating sales from day one.

  • Product Page Setup: We'll create pages for your products with images, descriptions, and pricing.
  • Secure Payment Integration: We integrate trusted payment gateways like Razorpay or Stripe to accept payments safely.
  • Easy Order Management: You'll get a simple dashboard to track orders, manage inventory, and view your sales.

Basic SEO & Fast Performance

A beautiful website is useless if no one can find it. We build every site with basic Search Engine Optimization (SEO) in mind to help you get found on Google. We also ensure your site loads quickly to keep visitors happy.

  • On-Page SEO Setup: We optimize your titles, descriptions, and content to be search-engine-friendly.
  • Keyword Targeting: We help identify and include the right keywords to attract your target audience.
  • Speed Optimization: We make sure your website is fast, which is crucial for both users and Google rankings.

Website Redesign & Redevelopment

Is your current website feeling a bit outdated? We breathe new life into old sites. Whether it's a visual makeover or a complete technical overhaul, we transform slow, aging websites into modern, high-performance business tools.

  • Modern UI/UX Refresh: We update your look to match current design trends and improve user flow.
  • Platform Migration: Moving from outdated systems to modern, manageable platforms like WordPress.
  • Performance Boost: We clean up old code to make your site faster and more secure for today's web.

Social Media Management

Your customers are on social media—you should be too. We help startups build a consistent brand presence across Instagram, LinkedIn, and Facebook, so you can focus on running your business while we handle the engagement.

  • Content Calendar Planning: We plan your posts in advance so your brand never goes silent.
  • Engagement & Growth: We help grow your followers and respond to comments to build a loyal community.
  • Targeted Ad Campaigns: Setting up budget-friendly ads to reach local customers in Gurugram and beyond.

Professional Video Editing

Video is the most powerful way to tell your story. From snappy Instagram Reels to professional corporate explainers, we edit your raw footage into high-impact content that grabs attention instantly.

  • Short-Form Content: Expert editing for Reels, TikToks, and YouTube Shorts that are optimized for the algorithm.
  • Corporate & Promo Videos: Professional polishing for product launches, testimonials, and brand stories.
  • Motion Graphics: Adding text animations, transitions, and effects to make your videos stand out.

Graphic & Brand Design

A great business needs a great look. We provide comprehensive design services to ensure your brand looks professional across all touchpoints, from your logo to your marketing flyers.

  • Logo & Identity Design: Creating unique logos that represent your business values and vision.
  • Marketing Collateral: Design for brochures, business cards, flyers, and digital banners.
  • Social Media Graphics: Custom-designed templates for your posts to keep your feed looking premium.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long will it take to build my website?

Our process is fast! A Starter Plan website is typically ready in **7-10 days**. Business and Premium plans usually take around **2-3 weeks**, depending on your specific needs.

Do you work with clients outside of Gurugram?

Absolutely. While we are proudly based in Gurugram, we serve clients all across India. We use phone calls, video chats, and email to make collaboration easy, no matter where you are located.

What kind of support do you offer after launch?

Our Business and Premium plans come with **1-2 months of free support** to fix any issues after launch. For long-term peace of mind, we also offer very affordable annual maintenance plans.

Can you help redesign my existing website?

Yes, definitely! We can give your old website a modern and professional makeover at a very competitive price. Contact us with details about your current site for a custom quote.
