Reliable Website Maintenance & Support

Don't let a broken or slow website cost you customers. We keep your digital presence secure, updated, backed up, and running at peak performance so you can focus on running your business.

Complete Protection & Upkeep

Proactive care to ensure your website remains an asset, not a liability.

Proactive Security & Monitoring

The internet is full of bots and bad actors looking for vulnerabilities. We provide enterprise-grade security monitoring to keep hackers out and your customer data safe.

  • 24/7 Uptime Monitoring: We know the second your site goes down, and we jump in to fix it immediately.
  • Malware Scanning: Regular deep scans to detect and remove malicious code before it causes harm.
  • Firewall Configuration: Setting up robust Web Application Firewalls (WAF) to block suspicious traffic.

Software & Plugin Updates

Outdated software is the number one cause of website hacks and broken layouts. We safely manage all the technical updates happening behind the scenes.

  • CMS Core Updates: Safely updating WordPress, Shopify, or custom framework cores to the latest stable versions.
  • Plugin & Theme Management: Testing and updating third-party add-ons without breaking your site's functionality.
  • PHP & Database Upgrades: Ensuring your server environment meets modern performance and security standards.

Regular Backups & Recovery

Disasters happen—whether it's a server crash or a human error deleting a crucial page. Our automated backup systems ensure you never lose your hard work or data.

  • Automated Daily/Weekly Backups: Scheduled secure backups stored safely off-site in the cloud.
  • Full Site Copies: We back up everything—your database, your images, your files, and your code.
  • 1-Click Restoration: If something goes wrong, we can roll back your website to a working version in minutes.

Speed & Performance Optimization

A slow website frustrates users and hurts your Google rankings. We continuously optimize your website to ensure it loads in the blink of an eye.

  • Image Compression: Automatically optimizing large media files to reduce page weight.
  • Caching Configuration: Setting up advanced browser and server caching for instant page loads.
  • Database Cleanup: Regularly clearing out spam comments, post revisions, and bloated database tables.

Content Updates & Bug Fixes

Need to swap an image, add a new team member, or fix a broken button? Don't stress over the technical details. Just send us an email, and we'll handle the updates for you.

  • On-Demand Webmaster Support: A dedicated amount of time each month for your custom edit requests.
  • CSS & Layout Tweaks: Fixing alignment issues, mobile responsiveness problems, or broken UI elements.
  • Content Publishing: Send us your blog posts or new product descriptions, and we'll format and publish them beautifully.

Maintenance FAQs

Why do I need a website maintenance plan?

Websites are software, and like any software (like the apps on your phone), they need regular updates to function securely. Without maintenance, your site becomes vulnerable to hacks, plugins break, loading speeds drop, and you risk losing your search engine rankings.

Do you maintain websites that you didn't build?

Yes, absolutely. Before onboarding, we perform a comprehensive "Website Health Check" to understand how it was built, identify any existing issues, and ensure we can safely bring it under our maintenance umbrella.

How do I request a content update or bug fix?

It's simple! You just send an email to our dedicated support desk. Most minor updates (text changes, swapping images, adding a blog post) are completed within 24 to 48 business hours.

What happens if my website gets hacked?

If you are on one of our premium maintenance plans, our security systems will likely block the attempt. However, if a breach does occur, we will immediately clean the malware, restore your site from a clean backup, and harden the security—at no extra cost to you.
