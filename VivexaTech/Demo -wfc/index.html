Our Story

More Than a Club, We're a Sisterhood.

Discover the story behind WFC, born from a simple idea: to create a dedicated space for women to connect authentically, grow fearlessly, and celebrate life's moments, big and small.

The WFC Genesis

Woman Fun Club started with a conversation between friends. We realized that in our busy lives, juggling careers, families, and personal goals, it was becoming harder to find meaningful, supportive friendships—the kind that lift you up and make you feel understood.

We craved a community that was less about networking and more about connecting; a place where we could be our true selves, share our passions, learn new things, and just have fun without any pressure. Unable to find such a space, we decided to build it ourselves. WFC was founded to be that haven for every woman seeking genuine connection, personal empowerment, and pure, unadulterated fun.

Our Mission & Core Values

Our principles guide everything we do, ensuring WFC is always a positive and enriching space.

Connect

To foster a warm, inclusive environment where women from all backgrounds can build deep, lasting friendships and a powerful support system.

Empower

To provide resources, workshops, and experiences that inspire confidence, encourage personal growth, and support professional ambitions.

Celebrate

To create moments of joy, laughter, and fun. We believe in celebrating every woman's journey and achievements, making life more vibrant.

Riya Mehta

Founder & Chief Community Builder

"My dream was to build more than just a club; it was to weave a tapestry of diverse, brilliant women who could come together to share, support, and simply 'be'. WFC is that dream come true."

Riya is a passionate community advocate with a background in brand marketing and event management. After years of organizing large-scale corporate events, she wanted to create something with more heart. She envisioned a community where every event felt like a gathering of close friends. Today, she dedicates her time to curating unique experiences for WFC members and ensuring that the spirit of connection remains at the core of everything we do.
