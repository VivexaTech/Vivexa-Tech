Open Internship Roles (2026 Batch)

SEO Intern

Dive into the world of Search Engine Optimization. You will help us rank client websites on Google, ensuring maximum digital visibility.

  • Keyword Research: Identify high-traffic search terms for specific business niches.
  • On-Page Optimization: Learn to optimize meta tags, content, and website structure.
  • Performance Tracking: Monitor traffic growth and generate basic analytics reports.

Calling & Outreach Intern

Become the voice of Vivexa Tech. This role is perfect for outgoing students who want to develop exceptional communication, pitching, and sales skills.

  • Client Communication: Reach out to potential clients to explain our web services.
  • Lead Nurturing: Follow up with prospects and maintain a healthy pipeline.
  • Relationship Building: Learn how to understand client pain points and offer effective digital solutions.

Data Research Intern

Data is the fuel for our growth. If you are analytical, detail-oriented, and love finding patterns on the web, this role is for you.

  • Market Research: Gather actionable data on local businesses and industry competitors.
  • Lead Generation: Build accurate databases of potential clients for our outreach team.
  • Trend Analysis: Keep track of new digital marketing trends and summarize findings.

Internship FAQs

Who is eligible to apply?

We are exclusively looking for current college students (freshers/2026 batch) who are hungry to learn. If you are dedicated and have reliable internet access, you can apply!

Are these internships strictly Work-From-Home?

Yes! We know you have college classes to attend, so all our internships are 100% remote. You can work from anywhere.

How long is the internship duration?

Our standard internship programs run for **2 to 3 months**. Exceptional performers may be offered extended roles or freelance opportunities.

What is the selection process?

Once you fill out the application form, we will review your details. If shortlisted, you will be invited for a quick virtual introductory call to discuss the role and your interests.
