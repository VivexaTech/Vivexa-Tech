Admission Process

Our admission process is designed to be transparent and straightforward. We welcome applications from students of all backgrounds. Please follow the steps below:

  1. Submit Enquiry Form: Fill out the contact form on our website or visit the school office.
  2. Campus Tour & Interaction: Schedule a visit to our campus and have an interaction with our admissions counselor.
  3. Application Form: Purchase and submit the completed application form along with the required documents.
  4. Assessment: A simple, age-appropriate assessment will be conducted for prospective students.
  5. Offer of Admission: Successful candidates will receive an offer of admission. Complete the fee payment to secure the seat.
